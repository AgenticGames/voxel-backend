Geological Time Simulation

Simulates 1.25 million years of geological processes: metamorphism, hydrothermal veins, contact aureoles, acid dissolution, and structural collapse.

Tip: Switch the Tool to "Place Lava" and click the cave walls to inject magma. Lava creates contact aureoles — heat transforms surrounding limestone into marble and skarn, slate into hornfels, and deposits garnet and diopside. Place water near lava to form hydrothermal pockets that drive vein mineralization.
